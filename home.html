AI-Powered 2D to 3D Model Converter

This AI-driven project uses **Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN)** to transform **2D floor plans** into **high-quality 3D models**. Powered by **computer vision** and deep learning, it generates accurate 3D reconstructions from simple images.

Key Features

  • 📌 AI & CNN-powered 3D model generation
  • 📌 Converts **2D floor plans** into **3D objects**
  • 📌 Interactive **3D viewer** with zoom and rotation
  • 📌 Fast & easy **upload and process** interface
